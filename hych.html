How you can help

  • Donate towards ongoing expenses.
  • Volunteer - We have a wide range of positions for trapping, feeding
    fundraising, fostering, and administration. To Volunteer complete the form and send it to campbellriverpaws@gmail.com.
    or print it, fill it out, and mail it to CR PAWS PO BOx 201 Campbell River, BC V9W 5A7.
    Our volunteer coordinator will get in touch with you once your application is recieved and reviewed.
  • Donate Supplies, we always need:
    Cat food (kibble and canned). Higher quality is best but will accept any donations, even open bags.
    Kitten Milk Replacers
    And Cat Litter, Unscented and preferrably non clumping.
  • Donations of gas gift cards in small denominations.
  • Return your refundable cans and bottles to Island Return It Depot (located on Willow St. and 16th Ave.) and donate the refund to the CR paws account.
  • Follow us on our lost and found animals of campbell river facebook page and help search for or report sightings of lost pets.
  • Spread the word and help educate people about feral cat colonies and lost pets.
