Our Mission

To ensure the safety and care of animals in Campbell River through the use of knowledge and expertise.
We also delve into the recovering lost pets, and the ongoing task of helping community and feral cats including a process called TNR.
TNR stands for Trap, Neuter, Return. Lastly we also work in tandem with other rescue organizations.
